SOFIYA

Sofiya Loriashvili is a contemporary artist who focuses on the intersection of painting, digital art, and experimental visual narratives. Her work often explores the transformation of classical forms through modern technical lenses, incorporating elements of mysticism, architectural rhythm, and anatomical deconstruction. Loriashvili’s practice is characterized by a sophisticated use of color and texture, blending traditional techniques with a conceptual approach to space and human identity.
